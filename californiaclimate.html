How Climate Change in California May Affect Its Housing Prices

Increased temperatures lead to:

  • higher likelihood of forest fires
  • greater electricity usage
  • decreased air quality

Increased sea levels decrease the amount of available land for housing:

Climate change has decreased the amount of available land for housing in California and thus homebuyers are willing to pay more for available housing, so sellers can raise real estate prices and sell them for more than they are actually worth.
