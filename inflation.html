Inflation in the United States

About the US Dollar

  • With the exception of 2009, the US dollar has inflated every year since 1972.
  • According to the website in2013dollars, the US dollar has inflated by a total of 600% over the past 50 years.
  • $100 in 1972, adjusted for inflation, is worth roughly $700 in 2022.
