Global Climate Change is Getting Worse

General Trends of Global Climate Change

Earth’s average temperature has sharply risen over the past 50 years

  • Earth's surface temperature has increased by almost 3°F since 1850
    • Primarily caused by human-related activities and CO2 emissions
    • Most of the warming occured within the last 50 years
  • Much of this heat is absorbed by the oceans, which leads to increased sea levels
    • Arctic ice sheets have been melting at increasing rates (which also leads to rising sea levels)
