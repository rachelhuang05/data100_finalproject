Housing Price Trends

Housing Prices' Changes Over the Last 50 Years

  • Adjusted for inflation, the median house price in the US has quintupled over the last 50 years
  • From mid-2016 to 2024, the median house price in California has increased by almost 50%
  • The current US median house price is still 15% lower than the California median was almost a decade ago
  • Since 2016, the California median house price has been anywhere from 133% to 194% of the national median
